When I was a little girl, I used to help my grandmother bake cookies and decorate our home before holidays. I dreamed to become a famous singer than and to give concerts around the world. My life was supposed to be unusual and fascinating. These were days when everyone was crazy about disco and ABBA. Now I'm 46. Well, I did not become a singer, but home decoration and baking are still my favourite weekend activities. Nevertheless, during last few decades I managed to live in different cities, visit many interesting countries, meet a lot of (wonderful) people and to learn several languages; I was performing some technological design in AutoCAD, was selling equipment for restaurants and purchasing equipment for subway, was working as a personal assistant, volunteered at a children's hockey school, made a career in transportation and logistics in order to, as it turned out, quit it all up and move to another country, where start all over again... with a new passion for web development and my eyes wide open for the new professional opportunities.

With best wishes for a brilliant Christmas,
Natalia Petelina
